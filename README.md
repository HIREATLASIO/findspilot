# FindsPilot

FindsPilot is an independent China-shopping agent comparison, marketplace-link converter and evidence-led buying guide. The public continuity site is live at [hireatlasio.github.io/findspilot](https://hireatlasio.github.io/findspilot/).

## What is live

- Neutral official routes for JoyaGoo, Superbuy, AllChinaBuy and KakoBuy
- Client-side Taobao, Weidian, 1688 and Tmall link validation
- An indicative shipping-range calculator
- Fixed-scope partner offers from €150 to €600
- A structured public partner-intake form
- Affiliate, editorial, shopping-risk and privacy disclosures
- A phone-persistent owner launch checklist
- A separate mobile Launch OS with revenue readiness, controlled signups, truthful commission math, service-sales math and original content briefs

## Commercial status

No commission route is active in this deployment. Official links remain neutral until a fresh company-owned affiliate account is approved, its terms are recorded and the outbound route passes a conversion test. Sponsorship cannot buy rankings or remove evidence and safety notes.

## Continuity scope

This repository contains the zero-cost static continuity deployment created after the full Vercel application was disabled at the hosting-account level. The full application—including the larger screened catalog, owner operations dashboards, attribution ledger and server-side route controls—remains in the private workspace.

## Safety and privacy

The public catalog strategy excludes records with unverified protected marks or replica-risk language. The static site has no account system, analytics pixel or form database. Partner intake is public and must not contain credentials, customer data, private contracts or payout information.
